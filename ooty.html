About Ooty

Nestled amidst the majestic Nilgiri Hills, Ooty is known as the "Queen of Hill Stations" in South India. This charming destination is renowned for its lush green landscapes, sprawling tea gardens, and breathtaking views. Visitors are captivated by the serene Ooty Lake, the vibrant Botanical Gardens, and the panoramic vistas from Doddabetta Peak. The pleasant climate throughout the year makes it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and honeymooners alike.

Ooty is not only famous for its scenic beauty but also for its rich colonial history and heritage. The Nilgiri Mountain Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage site, offers an unforgettable journey through misty mountains and dense forests. The bustling local markets offer a variety of handmade chocolates, aromatic spices, and traditional handicrafts. Whether it's a leisurely boat ride on the lake or a stroll through the tea plantations, Ooty promises an enchanting experience for every traveler.
